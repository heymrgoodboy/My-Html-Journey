Ritesh Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 Cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cups bread flour

For the filing:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar ,eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the Mixture
  3. Add the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Kenad the dough for 10 Minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic Wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 Hour.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon
  7. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
